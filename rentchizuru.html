Appearance

Chizuru has waist-length chestnut-brown hair that is most notably tied with bangs hanging on the right side and light brown eyes. When posing as a diligent student, she wears glasses, a butterfly accessory and she sports double braided hair. It is later revealed that she does have sight trouble and she uses a pair of contact lenses when she is not going out with her friends. Her presentation is represented as being a madonnna; possessing a beauty that one can only ever dream of having; light clothing, makeup, and accessories.

Personality

At a glance, Chizuru puts up a kind and considerate front: It surprises anyone who considers renting, which makes them give high ratings to her. However, she only does this to her customers. In reality, she is sharp, brash, and calculative; showing her true self to people she dislikes or has to put up with. However, she does display a genuine kinder side when she sees people in need.
